Post this from your **personal account** (not the @FeeEdge brand handle). First-person,

---

**1/**
I kept switching crypto exchanges and never actually knew if I was saving money or just moving it around.

So last month I sat down and compared the fees on every venue I use. The result annoyed me enough that I built a tool. 🧵

**2/**
Turns out "which exchange is cheapest" has no single answer.

It depends on your volume, whether you're mostly maker or taker, perps vs spot, even which coins you trade. The "cheapest" exchange for me was not the cheapest for a friend doing 10x my size.

**3/**
When I ran my real numbers across 20 exchanges, the spread between the cheapest and the most expensive came out to **four figures a year** — for identical trading.

I'd just… never checked. I suspect most people haven't either.

**4/**
The rabbit hole got deeper. The real cost isn't just the maker/taker number:
• funding on perps
• native-token discounts (BNB, OKB, KCS…)
• withdrawal fees per chain
• volume-tier breakpoints

So I built something that rolls all of it into one number per exchange.

**5/**
It's called FeeEdge. You put in how you trade, it ranks 20 exchanges by what you'd actually pay. ~10 seconds.

Free shows your 3 cheapest. There's a $29 one-time Pro if you want all 20 + the funding optimizer, alerts, and the deeper tools. No subscription — I hate those too.

**6/**
This is an early version and I'm building it in the open. If you try it:
• tell me if the cheapest venue surprises you
• tell me what's wrong or missing

👉 https://feeedge.com

Genuinely want the feedback. RTs help a ton while it's tiny. 🙏
