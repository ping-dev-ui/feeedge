# YouTube influencer outreach — FeeEdge

I can't message creators for you (and most contact details are gated on each
channel's About tab), but here's the targeting, the model, and ready-to-send copy.

---

## Strategy: who to target (and who to skip)
- **Best fit = trading / exchange-review channels**, not generic "crypto news." Your hook (fees) lands with people actively *trading* and choosing venues.
- **Start with mid-size & small channels (10k–300k subs)**, on a **performance/affiliate basis** — they reply, they convert well, and there's near-zero cash risk. Mega channels (Coin Bureau, Benjamin Cowen) charge large **flat** sponsorship fees — revisit only with a budget.
- **The ideal video context:** "best crypto exchange / lowest fees / exchange tier review / how to save on trading fees" — FeeEdge slots in as the tool that proves the point.

## How to get their contact
1. Channel → **About** tab → **"View email address"** (may show a CAPTCHA). That business email is the right channel.
2. If no email: **links section** often has a business-inquiry form, Telegram, or X — DM there.
3. Bigger channels list a **management/agency** ("for business: …") — email the agency.

## The model (low cash risk, performance-first)
1. **Gauge interest first** with a short note — don't lead with money or a wall of terms.
2. If interested, offer:
   - a **free Pro code** (unique, 100%-off, **capped to 1 redemption**) so they can try it,
   - a **unique `?ref=` link** for tracking,
   - a **flat bounty per paid signup** (e.g. ~$8) — pay only on *paid* sales, stated up front.
3. For a creator who wants a flat fee for a dedicated segment, negotiate — but anchor on performance first.
4. Independence still holds: they can say whatever they want; FeeEdge ranks by real cost regardless.

## Practical notes
- **Personalize line 1** to a specific recent video of theirs ("loved your Bybit fee breakdown…") — massively lifts reply rate.
- Send a **unique code + `?ref=` per creator** so attribution is clean (the `?ref=` → Stripe metadata pipeline already tracks this; check `/admin`).
- Track which creators convert in PostHog by `ref`; double down on the ones that work.
- Comp codes are **capped to 1 redemption** so a leaked code can't give away free Pro at scale.
- Don't spam — 5–10 well-chosen, personalized notes beat 100 blasts.
